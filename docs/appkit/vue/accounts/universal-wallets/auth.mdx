# Auth

AppKit SDK enables passwordless Web3 onboarding and authentication, allowing your users interact with your application by creating a non-custodial wallet with just their email or social accounts. We support the following providers: Email, Google, X, GitHub, Discord, Apple and Facebook (coming soon)
